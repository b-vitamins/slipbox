#+TITLE: Ising model in a field
#+FILETAGS: :problem:main:spof:
#+SETUPFILE: ../../setupfile.org

Ising model in a field: Consider the partition function for the Ising model (\sigma_{i}= \pm 1) on a square lattice, in a magnetic field h; i.e.

\begin{align*}
Z=\sum_{\left\{\sigma_{i}\right\}} \exp \left
[
K \sum_{\langle i j\rangle} \sigma_{i} \sigma_{j}+h \sum_{i} \sigma_{i}\right]
\end{align*}
