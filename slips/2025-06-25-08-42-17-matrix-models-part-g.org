* Problem Statement

Use perturbation theory to calculate the coarse grained coupling constant $\tilde{K}$. Evaluate only the two diagrams that directly renormalize the $\left(\nabla \pi_{i j}\right)^{2}$ term in $\beta \mathcal{H}$, and show that

\begin{align*}
\tilde{K}=K+\frac{N}{2} \int_{\Lambda / b}^{\Lambda} \frac{\mathrm{d}^{d} \mathbf{q}}{(2 \pi)^{d}} \frac{1}{q^{2}}
\end{align*}
