#+TITLE: Position-space renormalization group
#+FILETAGS: :problem:main:spof:
#+SETUPFILE: ../../setupfile.org

Position-space renormalization group (PSRG): In the Migdal-Kadanoff approximation, a decimation is performed after bonds are moved to connect the remaining sites in a one-dimensional geometry. For a PSRG rescaling factor b on a d-dimensional hypercubic lattice, the retained sites are connected by b^{d-1} strands in parallel, each strand having b bonds in series. This scheme is naturally exact for d=1, and becomes progressively worse in higher dimensions.
