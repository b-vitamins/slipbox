* Part (a) Problem
By looking at diagrams in a second order perturbation expansion in both \(u\) and \(v\) show that the recursion relations for these couplings are

\begin{align*}
\left\{\begin{array}{l}
\frac{\mathrm{d} u}{\mathrm{~d} \ell}=\varepsilon u-4 C\left[(n+8) u^{2}+6 u v\right] \\
\frac{\mathrm{d} v}{\mathrm{~d} \ell}=\varepsilon v-4 C\left[12 u v+9 v^{2}\right]
\end{array}\right.
\end{align*}
where \(C=K_{d} \Lambda^{d} /\left(t+K \Lambda^{2}\right)^{2} \approx K_{4} / K^{2}\) is approximately a constant.
