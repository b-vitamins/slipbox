* Problem Statement

Prove that

\begin{align*}
\left\langle|h(\mathbf{x})-h(\mathbf{y})|^{2}\right\rangle=-\left.\frac{\mathrm{d}^{2}}{\mathrm{~d} k^{2}} G_{k}(\mathbf{x}-\mathbf{y})\right|_{k=0}
\end{align*}

where $G_{k}(\mathbf{x}-\mathbf{y})=\langle\exp 
[
\mathrm{i} k(h(\mathbf{x})-h(\mathbf{y}))]\rangle$.
