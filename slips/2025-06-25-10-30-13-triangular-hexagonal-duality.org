* Part (d) Problem
The triangular and hexagonal lattices are dual to each other. Combined with the star-triangle transformation that also maps the two lattices, this leads to exact values of p_{c} for these lattices. However, this calculation is not trivial, and it is best to follow the steps for calculations of the critical couplings of q-state Potts models in an earlier problem.
