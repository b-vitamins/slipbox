* Problem Statement

Calculate the position of the fixed point for the finite temperature phase transition.

* Context

This is part (a) of problem 8-6 on The XY model in 2+epsilon dimensions from Kardar's Statistical Physics of Fields.

The recursion relations are:
\begin{align*}
\left\{\begin{array}{l}
\frac{\mathrm{d} T}{\mathrm{~d} \ell}=-\epsilon T+4 \pi^{3} y^{2} \\
\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} \ell}=\left(2-\frac{\pi}{T}\right) y
\end{array}\right.
\end{align*}
