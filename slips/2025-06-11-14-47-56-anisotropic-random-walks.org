#+TITLE: Anisotropic random walks
#+FILETAGS: :problem:main:spof:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

Anisotropic random walks: Consider the ensemble of all random walks on a square lattice starting at the origin (0,0). Each walk has a weight of t_{x}^{\ell_{x}} \times t_{y}^{\ell_{y}}, where \ell_{x} and \ell_{y} are the number of steps taken along the x and y directions, respectively.
