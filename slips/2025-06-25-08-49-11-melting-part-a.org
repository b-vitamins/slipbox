* Problem Statement

Express the energy in terms of the Fourier transforms $u_{i}(\mathbf{q})$, and find the normal modes of vibrations.

* Context

This is part (a) of problem 8-9 on Melting from Kardar's Statistical Physics of Fields.

The elastic energy cost of a deformation $u_{i}(\mathbf{x})$ of an isotropic lattice is:
\begin{align*}
-\beta \mathcal{H}=\frac{1}{2} \int \mathrm{d}^{d} \mathbf{x}\left[
2 \mu u_{i j} u_{i j}+\lambda u_{i i} u_{j j}\right]
\end{align*}

where $u_{i j}(\mathbf{x})=\left(\partial_{i} u_{j}+\partial_{j} u_{i}\right) / 2$ is the strain tensor.
