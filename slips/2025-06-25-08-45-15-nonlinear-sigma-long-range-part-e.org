* Problem Statement

A simplifying feature of long-range interactions is that the coarse grained coupling constant is not modified by the perturbation, i.e. $\tilde{K}=K$ to all orders in a perturbative calculation. Use this information, along with simple dimensional analysis, to express the renormalized interaction, $K^{\prime}(b)$, in terms of $K, b$, and $\zeta$.
