#+TITLE: Nonlinear sigma model with long-range interactions
#+FILETAGS: :problem:main:spof:
#+SETUPFILE: ../../setupfile.org

Nonlinear \sigma model with long-range interactions: Consider unit $n$-component spins, $\vec{s}(\mathbf{x})=\left(s_{1}, s_{2}, \cdots, s_{n}\right)$ with $|\vec{s}(\mathbf{x})|^{2}=\sum_{i} s_{i}(\mathbf{x})^{2}=1$, interacting via a Hamiltonian

\begin{align*}
\beta \mathcal{H}=\int \mathrm{d}^{d} \mathbf{x} \int \mathrm{d}^{d} \mathbf{y} K(|\mathbf{x}-\mathbf{y}|) \vec{s}(\mathbf{x}) \cdot \vec{s}(\mathbf{y})
\end{align*}
