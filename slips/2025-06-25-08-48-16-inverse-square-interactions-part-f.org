* Problem Statement

Derive the effective interaction given above as Eq. (1). (Hint: This is somewhat easier than the corresponding calculation for the two-dimensional Coulomb gas, as the charges along the chain must alternate.)

* Context

This is part (f) of problem 8-8 on Inverse-square interactions from Kardar's Statistical Physics of Fields.

The effective coupling to derive is:
\begin{align*}
K \rightarrow K_{\mathrm{eff}}=K-2 K y_{0}^{2} \int_{a}^{\infty} \mathrm{d} r r\left(\frac{a}{r}\right)^{4 K}+\mathcal{O}\left(y_{0}^{4}\right)
\end{align*}
