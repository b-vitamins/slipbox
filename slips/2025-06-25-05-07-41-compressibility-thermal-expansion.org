* Part (c) Problem
The isothermal compressibility \(\kappa_{T}=\partial \rho /\left.\partial P\right|_{T} / \rho\), and the thermal expansion coefficient \(\alpha=\partial V /\left.\partial T\right|_{P} / V\).

Find the exponents \(x\) and \(y\) for the leading singular behavior of the form \(t^{x}\) on approaching the critical point along the isochore \(\rho=\rho_{c}\); or \(\delta \rho^{y}\) for a path along the isotherm \(T=T_{c}\).

Check that your results for parts (b) and (c) are consistent with the thermodynamic identity \(C_{P}-C_{V}=T V \alpha^{2} / \kappa_{T}\).
