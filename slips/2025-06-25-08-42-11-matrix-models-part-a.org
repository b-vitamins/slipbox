* Problem Statement

Rewrite the Hamiltonian and the orthogonality constraint in terms of the matrix elements $M_{i j}(i, j=1, \cdots, N)$. Describe the ground state of the system.

* Context

This is part (a) of problem 8-2 on Matrix models from Kardar's Statistical Physics of Fields.

The Hamiltonian is:
\begin{align*}
\beta \mathcal{H}=\frac{K}{4} \int \mathrm{d}^{d} \mathbf{x} \operatorname{tr}\left[
\nabla M(\mathbf{x}) \cdot \nabla M^{T}(\mathbf{x})\right]
\end{align*}

where $M$ is a real, $N \times N$ orthogonal matrix with condition $M M^{T}=M^{T} M=I$.
