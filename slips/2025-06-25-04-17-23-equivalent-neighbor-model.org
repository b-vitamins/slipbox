* Part (a) Problem
For \(N\) spins we make the drastic approximation that the interaction between all spins is the same, and \(J_{i j}=-J / N\) (the equivalent neighbor model). Show that the energy can now be written as \(E(M, h)=-N\left[Jm^{2} / 2+h m\right]\), with a magnetization \(m=\sum_{i=1}^{N} \sigma_{i} / N=M / N\).
