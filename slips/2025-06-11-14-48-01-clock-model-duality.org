#+TITLE: Clock model duality
#+FILETAGS: :problem:main:spof:
#+SETUPFILE: ../../setupfile.org

Clock model duality: Consider spins s_{i}=(1,2, \cdots, q) placed on the sites of a square lattice, interacting via the clock model Hamiltonian

\begin{align*}
\beta \mathcal{H}_{C}=-\sum_{\langle i, j\rangle} J\left(\left|s_{i}-s_{j}\right|_{\bmod q}\right)
\end{align*}
