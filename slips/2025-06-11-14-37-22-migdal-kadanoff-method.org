Migdal-Kadanoff method: Consider Potts spins \(s_{i}=(1,2, \cdots, q)\), on sites \(i\) of a hypercubic lattice, interacting with their nearest neighbors via a Hamiltonian

\
[
-\beta \mathcal{H}=K \sum_{\langle i j\rangle} \delta_{s_{i}, s_{j}}\]
