#+TITLE: Potts duality
#+FILETAGS: :problem:main:spof:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

Potts duality: Consider Potts spins, s_{i}=(1,2, \cdots, q), placed on the sites of a square lattice of N sites, interacting with their nearest-neighbors through a Hamiltonian

\begin{align*}
-\beta \mathcal{H}=K \sum_{\langle i j\rangle} \delta_{s_{i}, s_{j}}
\end{align*}
