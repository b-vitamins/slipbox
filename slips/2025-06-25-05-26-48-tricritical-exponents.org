* Part (d) Problem
The special point \(u=t=0\), separating first and second order phase boundaries, is a tricritical point. For \(u=0\), calculate the tricritical exponents \(\beta, \delta, \gamma\), and \(\alpha\), governing the singularities in magnetization, susceptibility, and heat capacity. (Recall: \(C \propto t^{-\alpha} ; \bar{m}(h=0) \propto t^{\beta} ; \chi \propto t^{-\gamma} ;\) and \(\left.\bar{m}(t=0) \propto h^{1 / \delta}.\right)\)
