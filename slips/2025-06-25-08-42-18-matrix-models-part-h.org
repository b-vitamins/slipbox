* Problem Statement

Using the result from part (f), show that after matrix rescaling, the RG equation for $K^{\prime}$ is given by:

\begin{align*}
K^{\prime}=b^{d-2}\left[
K-\frac{N-2}{2} \int_{\Lambda / b}^{\Lambda} \frac{\mathrm{d}^{d} \mathbf{q}}{(2 \pi)^{d}} \frac{1}{q^{2}}\right] .
\end{align*}
