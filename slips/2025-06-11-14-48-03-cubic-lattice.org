Cubic lattice: The geometric concept of duality can be extended to general dimensions d. However, the dual of a geometric element of dimension D is an entity of dimension d-D. For example, the dual of a bond (D=1) in d=3 is a plaquette (D=2), as demonstrated in this problem.
