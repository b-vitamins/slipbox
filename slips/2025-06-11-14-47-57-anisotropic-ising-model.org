#+TITLE: Anisotropic ising model
#+FILETAGS: :problem:main:spof:
#+SETUPFILE: ../../setupfile.org

Anisotropic ising model: Consider the anisotropic Ising model on a square lattice with a Hamiltonian

\begin{align*}
-\beta \mathcal{H}=\sum_{x, y}\left(K_{x} \sigma_{x, y} \sigma_{x+1, y}+K_{y} \sigma_{x, y} \sigma_{x, y+1}\right)
\end{align*}

i.e. with bonds of different strengths along the x and y directions.
