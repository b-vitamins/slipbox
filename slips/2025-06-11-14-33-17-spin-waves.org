#+TITLE: Spin Waves
#+FILETAGS: :problem:main:spof:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

Spin waves: In the XY model of \(n=2\) magnetism, a unit vector \(\vec{s}=\left(s_{x}, s_{y}\right)\) (with \(s_{x}^{2}+s_{y}^{2}=1\) ) is placed on each site of a \(d\)-dimensional lattice. There is an interaction that tends to keep nearest-neighbors parallel, i.e. a Hamiltonian

\begin{align*}
-\beta \mathcal{H}=K \sum_{\langle i j\rangle} \vec{s}_{i} \cdot \vec{s}_{j}
\end{align*}

The notation \(\langle i j\rangle\) is conventionally used to indicate summing over all nearestneighbor pairs \((i, j)\).
