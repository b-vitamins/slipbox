#+TITLE: Long-range interactions
#+FILETAGS: :problem:main:spof:
#+SETUPFILE: ../../setupfile.org

Long-range interactions: Consider a continuous spin field \(\vec{s}(\mathbf{x})\), subject to a long-range ferromagnetic interaction

\begin{align*}
\int \mathrm{d}^{d} \mathbf{x} \mathrm{d}^{d} \mathbf{y} \frac{\vec{s}(\mathbf{x}) \cdot \vec{s}(\mathbf{y})}{|\mathbf{x}-\mathbf{y}|^{d+\sigma}}
\end{align*}

as well as short-range interactions.
