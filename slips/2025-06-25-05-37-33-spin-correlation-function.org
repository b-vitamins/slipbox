* Part (f) Problem
Find an expression for \(\left\langle\vec{s}_{0} \cdot \vec{s}_{\mathbf{x}}\right\rangle=\Re\left\langle\exp \left[\mathrm{i} \theta_{\mathbf{x}}-i \theta_{0}\right]\right\rangle\) by adding contributions from different Fourier modes. Convince yourself that for \(|\mathbf{x}| \rightarrow \infty\), only \(\mathbf{q} \rightarrow \mathbf{0}\) modes contribute appreciably to this expression, and hence calculate the asymptotic limit.
