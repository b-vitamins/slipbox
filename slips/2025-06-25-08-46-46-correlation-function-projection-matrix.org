* Part (d) Problem
Show that the correlation function can be calculated from

\[\left\langle\delta_{s_{i}, s_{i+\ell}}\right\rangle=\frac{1}{Z} \sum_{\alpha=1}^{q} \operatorname{tr}\left[\Pi_{\alpha} T^{\ell} \Pi_{\alpha} T^{N-\ell}\right]\]

where \(\Pi_{\alpha}\) is a projection matrix. Hence show that \(\left\langle\delta_{s_{i}, s_{i+\ell}}\right\rangle_{c} \sim [\lambda(1) / \lambda(0)]^{e}\). (You do not have to explicitly calculate the constant of proportionality.)
