#+FILETAGS: :problem:main:spof:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

Exponents: Two critical exponents at second order are,

\begin{align*}
\left\{\begin{array}{l}
\nu=\frac{1}{2}+\frac{(n+2)}{4(n+8)} \epsilon+\frac{(n+2)\left(n^{2}+23 n+60\right)}{8(n+8)^{3}} \epsilon^{2} \
\eta=\frac{(n+2)}{2(n+8)^{2}} \epsilon^{2}
\end{array}\right.
\end{align*}

Use scaling relations to obtain \(\epsilon\)-expansions for two or more of the remaining exponents \(\alpha, \beta, \gamma, \delta\) and \(\Delta\). Make a table of the results obtained by setting \(\epsilon=1,2\) for \(n=1,2\) and 3 ; and compare to the best estimates of these exponents that you can find by other sources (series, experiments, etc.).
