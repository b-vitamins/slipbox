The spin-1 model: Consider a linear chain where the spin \(s_{i}\) at each site takes on three values \(s_{i}=-1,0,+1\). The spins interact via a Hamiltonian

\
[
-\beta \mathcal{H}=\sum_{i} K s_{i} s_{i+1}\]
