#+TITLE: Fluctuations around a tricritical point
#+FILETAGS: :problem:main:spof:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

Fluctuations around a tricritical point: As shown in a previous problem, the Hamiltonian

\begin{align*}
\beta \mathcal{H}=\int \mathrm{d}^{d} \mathbf{x}\left
[
\frac{K}{2}(\nabla m)^{2}+\frac{t}{2} m^{2}+u m^{4}+v m^{6}\right]
\end{align*}

with \(u=0\) and \(v>0\) describes a tricritical point.
