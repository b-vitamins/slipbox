#+TITLE: The Potts model
#+FILETAGS: :problem:main:spof:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org


The Potts model: The transfer matrix procedure can be extended to the Potts models, where the spin \(s_{i}\) on each site takes \(q\) values \(s_{i}=(1,2, \cdots, q)\); and the Hamiltonian is \(-\beta \mathcal{H}=K \sum_{i=1}^{N} \delta_{s_{i}, s_{i+1}}+K \delta_{s_{N}, s_{1}}\).
