#+TITLE: The XY model in 2+epsilon dimensions
#+FILETAGS: :problem:main:spof:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

The XY model in 2+\epsilon dimensions: The recursion relations of the XY model in two dimensions can be generalized to $d=2+\epsilon$ dimensions, and take the form:

\begin{align*}
\left\{\begin{array}{l}
\frac{\mathrm{d} T}{\mathrm{~d} \ell}=-\epsilon T+4 \pi^{3} y^{2} \\
\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} \ell}=\left(2-\frac{\pi}{T}\right) y
\end{array}\right.
\end{align*}
