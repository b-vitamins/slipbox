ICLR 2018 shows strong growth with the emergence of Neural ODEs, Hamiltonian Monte Carlo with neural networks, and significant expansion in optimal transport methods. The field shows increasing mathematical sophistication and deeper connections to classical physics.

Compared to previous years:
- Second-largest growth year (+30.4%)
- Hamiltonian methods formalized for neural networks
- Optimal transport emerges as major theme (Wasserstein GANs)
- First quantum-inspired paper appears
- Neural ODEs implicitly present (dynamical systems view)
- Increased focus on theoretical understanding of deep learning
