ICLR 2017 introduces normalizing flows and significantly expands geometric/topological methods. While total count decreased slightly, the sophistication and diversity of physics-inspired methods increased dramatically. This year marks the emergence of flow-based generative models and systematic study of energy-based GANs.

Compared to previous years:
- Normalizing flows emerge (Density estimation using Real NVP)
- Energy-based GANs formalized
- Geometric and topological methods expand significantly
- Information-theoretic perspectives deepen (Deep Variational Information Bottleneck)
- Physics experiments via RL introduced
- More principled theoretical frameworks emerge
